Content

This dataset contains the various finance detail of India.

Aggregate expenditure.
Capital expenditure.
Social sector expenditure.
Revenue expenditure.
Revenue deficit.
Gross fiscal deficit.
Own tax revenues.
Nominal GSDP series.


Granularity: Annual
Time period: 1980-81 to 2015-16.
Amount: in crore rupees (i.e, 1 crore = 10 million)
